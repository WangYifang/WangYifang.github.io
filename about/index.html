Yifang Wang (王懿芳) is currently a PhD student in HKUST VisLab, at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), under the supervision of Prof. Qu Huamin. Before that, she obtained her B.Eng. degree from Zhejiang University majoring in Software Engineering in 2018. During her undergraduate studies, she worked in ZJUIDG Group at the State Key Lab of CAD & CG, supervised by Prof. Yingcai Wu and her work mainly focused on immersive visual analytics.

Her research interests include visual analytics of computational social science, digital humanities, and anomaly detection. She is currently looking for collaborators in social science and humanities to work together!
