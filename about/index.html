I’m Yifang Wang(王懿芳), an undergraduate student major in Software Engineering at Zhejiang University. I’m now a member of ZJUVIS Group at the State Key Lab of CAD & CG, supervised by Prof. Yingcai Wu.
My research interests include Immersive Visual Analytics and Information Visualization, especially the Visualization of Traditional Chinese History and Culture.
